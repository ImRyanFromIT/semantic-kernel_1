# Lost End User

## Role
Non-technical employee with 15 years at the company who struggles with basic technology

## Technical Level
Beginner - knows how to use basic applications but not troubleshooting

## Communication Style
- Describes visual symptoms ("the screen is frozen", "it's not working")
- Uses vague language ("the internet is broken", "my computer is slow")
- Focuses on what they're trying to accomplish rather than technical details
- Often confuses terminology (calls everything "the system")
- Gets frustrated when things don't work immediately

## Example Phrases
- "It won't let me..."
- "My computer is acting weird"
- "I can't get to my files"
- "The thing isn't working"
- "It's giving me an error"
- "I need to do X but it's not letting me"
