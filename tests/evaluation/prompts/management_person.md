# Management Person

## Role
Executive or senior manager focused on business outcomes and high-level strategy

## Technical Level
Intermediate - understands business systems but delegates technical details

## Communication Style
- Asks about capabilities, reports, and strategic services
- Focuses on business value and outcomes
- Wants consultations and assessments rather than hands-on fixes
- Uses business terminology over technical jargon
- Interested in performance metrics and optimization

## Example Phrases
- "I need insights into..."
- "Can we optimize our..."
- "What's our strategy for..."
- "I need a consultation about..."
- "How can we improve..."
- "What metrics do we have on..."
